Tools 04 & 06

ChemCalc & Enthalpy Calculator

Precision chemistry calculations with step-by-step breakdowns.

🧮
ChemCalc
Local Utility
  • Mole ↔ mass ↔ formula weight conversions
  • Molarity calculator (c = n/V)
  • Temperature: °C ↔ °F ↔ K
  • Volume & pressure unit converters
  • Integrated with 3D Viewer (MW auto-fill)
n = m / M  ·  c = n / V  ·  K = °C + 273.15
🔥
Enthalpy Calculator
NIST + PubChem
  • ΔH°rxn via Hess's Law
  • Parse any balanced chemical equation
  • Live ΔHf° lookup per molecule
  • Full step-by-step thermochemical breakdown
  • Endothermic / exothermic classification
ΔH°rxn = Σ ΔHf°(products) − Σ ΔHf°(reactants)

Docking
Explorer

  • Cartoon, surface & stick display modes

Design

UI / UX Engineering

Premium scientific aesthetics, modular architecture, and performance-first design.

Roadmap

Future Improvements

ChemHub is designed to evolve — here's what's next on the roadmap.

01
